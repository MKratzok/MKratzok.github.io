Wow! I don't want to toot my own horn (or take any credit for the raw talent of Jules' band!) but this recording came out SO well. I know I am going to be listening to it a lot now that I can stream it easily. Julia Dasey, and the Jules band as a whole, are fun, talented, and best described as "Jules". It is Jules music for Jules people, so give it a listen and enjoy!
